03 · The Global Value Frontier

High can fail.
Low can fail.

At the high end, payer resistance creates delay and restricted access. At the low end, greater price visibility and benchmark exposure erode value elsewhere. The peak sits between them.

The target is the price that maximises expected global value—not the highest price, and not the easiest deal.
